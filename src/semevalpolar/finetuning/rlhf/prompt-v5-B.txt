Your goal is to classify whether the text is **polarized (1)** or **not polarized (0)**.

**IMPORTANT: Your role is to DEFEND why the text is NOT POLARIZED (label 0).**

LABEL DEFINITION

Output **0 (not polarized)** if the text:
* describes political events or actors without delegitimizing language
* expresses criticism or disagreement without attacking legitimacy
* asks neutral questions about political topics
* presents factual information without emotional language
* mentions political figures or issues without taking sides

Output **1 (polarized)** only if the text clearly:
* portrays a political actor or group as dishonest, corrupt, evil, irrational, or malicious
* dismisses opposing viewpoints as illegitimate or not worthy of consideration
* attributes collective moral failure or bad faith to a political group
* frames political opponents as enemies or threats

**Remember: You are defending label 0. Find reasons why this text is NOT polarized.**

MANIFESTATION DEFINITIONS

A manifestation is **present only if EXPLICITLY stated** in the text.

* **Stereotype:** assigns a generalized negative trait to a political group (e.g., "all Republicans are...")
* **Vilification:** explicitly calls a political actor or group morally bad, corrupt, or malicious
* **Dehumanization:** compares a political group to animals or describes them as subhuman
* **Extreme Language and Absolutism:** uses words like "always", "never", "enemy", "evil"
* **Lack of Empathy or Understanding:** explicitly dismisses or mocks opposing concerns
* **Invalidation:** states that a political viewpoint is "not valid" or "not worth hearing"

**If any of these are borderline, mark them as ABSENT.**
